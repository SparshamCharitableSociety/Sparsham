Facilitate their journey to school

Be part of our vision to reinforce their path to school.

Kerala Government provides free education in both Malayalam and English medium from lower primary to higher secondary. Apart from that schools provides free meals to the children.Encouraging a child to school will help to get a proper meal as well as education. We support all the needs like transportation, dress, books and other fees. In some exceptional cases we may support the family requirements after getting approval from EC.

Kerala is famous for providing free basic education from lower primary to higher secondary with a much better educational environments. The financial issues and the family related issues are one of the key factors which causes school drop outs to an extend. Our main focus is to encourage the child to continue his studies by overcoming these economic constraints. We are trying to overcome these economic constraints by providing school-office stationery, bags, uniform and travel expenses. In exceptional cases, we support the family by providing financial assistance. We distribute these materials or donate an amount to our beneficiaries during the educational year.

Today, the medical expenses are keep on rising and sometimes we may meet people who needs a financial assistance to overcome his medical condition. We encourage our members and our friends to contribute directly to the person's bank account.

The natural calamities and the epidemic dieases may cause huge financial burden to the society. We are responsible for assisting the local government, state government and the central government to overcome this crisis. We encourage our members to contribute directly to the diaster funds managed by various government agencies. We also visit the camps and distribute the required items or donate these items to government approved collection centres.

We are thankful to you for joining with us by arranging the materials or transportation or raising the funds.Any of these activities helps the needy to overcome the situation and start again.
